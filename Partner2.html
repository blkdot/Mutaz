Cosette

Cosette is the exclusive PR and Marketing agency of Mutaz Barshim. Cosette has represented Mutaz Barshim since 2017, where they created the full branding and management. The agency provides commercial representation and management for the athlete, building the technical proposals for global brands and negotiating sponsorship and partnership contracts.
Personal appearances, website development/management, and content creation are managed by a specialist sports marketing team within the agency. The management and partnership with Mutaz Barshim continue to be a productive and commercial success. We look forward to extending this success globally.
